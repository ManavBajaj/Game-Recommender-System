Dishonored 2

Description:

*Dishonored 2* is a stealth action-adventure game developed by Arkane Studios. Set in the fictional empire of the Isles, players choose between Emily Kaldwin and Corvo Attano to reclaim the throne from a usurper. Use supernatural abilities, unique gadgets, and stealth or combat to define your playstyle.
