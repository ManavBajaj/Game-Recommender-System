*Resident Evil 4* is a survival horror game developed by Capcom. Follow Leon S. Kennedy as he battles his way through a rural village in Europe to rescue the U.S. President's daughter, encountering terrifying enemies and intense combat scenarios along the way.

Why Play This Game?

*Resident Evil 4* redefined the survival horror genre with its action-packed gameplay, iconic enemies, and gripping story. Its influence is seen in many games that followed, making it a must-play for fans of the genre.
