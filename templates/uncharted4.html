Uncharted 4: A Thief's End

Description:

*Uncharted 4: A Thief's End* is an action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog. The game follows Nathan Drake, a retired treasure hunter, as he embarks on one last adventure to uncover a legendary pirate treasure. Set against breathtaking backdrops ranging from dense jungles to ancient ruins, this gripping narrative explores themes of family, betrayal, and redemption.
